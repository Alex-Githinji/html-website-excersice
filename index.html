Welcome to Urban Haven Apartment

Urban Haven Apartments are apartments located in Murang'a, Kiharu.This Apartment offers rentals to student within Muranga university of Technology
There are variaties of house specification such as bedsitter,singlerooms,one bedroom and also two bedroom with different payment.

  • Free wifi except for those living in single rooms.
  • Free parking except for those living in single rooms and bedsitters.
  • On-site laundry @ Kshs.150 per basket.
  • Free gym except for those living in single rooms and bedsitters
  • Swimming pool - free for everyone
  • Elevator access - elevators for easy access to upper floors.
  • Free water for everyone.
  • Community theatre - free for everyone
